Introduction We are designing a biomimetic soft jellyfish robot to observe whether limiting anthropogenic vibrations caused by underwater observation robots will be less disturbing to seafloor marine life. To limit these vibrations, we will not use a motor to facilitate motion. Instead we are implementing an electromagnet to actuate several 3-bar linkages to cause jellyfish-like motion.
